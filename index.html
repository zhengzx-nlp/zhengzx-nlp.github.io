Biography

I'm a second-year PhD student in Natural Language Processing Group, Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University . My advisors are Prof. Jiajun Chen and Prof. Shujian Huang. I have been taking the successive master-doctor program of study for doctoral degree since September 2016. I am now working with Dr. Alexandra Birch on machine translation in ILCC, School of Informatics, the University of Edinburgh as a visiting student.

I have broad research interests in Natural Language Processing and Deep Learning. Recently I am focusing on neural machine translation (NMT), especially with auxiliary contextual information internally or externally. I am also interested in sentence encoding with deep representation.
